What instance variables should the Employee class have?

What do the instance variables represent? What are the types of those instance variables? What methods would you implement in your Employee class?

Employee ID: This is a unique identifier for each employee.

Type: String or int (depends on the format and requirements).
Name: The full name of the employee.

Type: String.
Date of Birth: The birthdate of the employee.

Type: Date or String.
Department: The department in which the employee works.

Type: String.
Position/Title: The job title or position of the employee.

Type: String.
Salary: The salary or hourly wage of the employee.

Type: Double or BigDecimal for precision.
Date of Joining: The date when the employee joined the company.

Type: Date or String.
Contact Information: Email address or phone number.

Type: String.
